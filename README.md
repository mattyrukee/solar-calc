# Solar System Sizing Calculator — Nigeria

A free, instant solar system sizing calculator designed specifically for Nigerian residential homes. Calculate the right battery bank, inverter, and solar panel requirements based on your appliances.

## Features

- **Comprehensive Appliance Database**: 70+ common Nigerian household appliances with pre-configured power ratings
- **Smart Calculations**: Automatically calculates battery capacity, inverter size, and solar panel requirements
- **Voltage Selection**: Support for 12V, 24V, and 48V systems (48V recommended)
- **Oversizing Toggle**: Optional 1.2× oversizing for battery, inverter, and panels
- **Panel Size Options**: Compare different solar panel sizes and configurations
- **Detailed Breakdown**: View energy consumption breakdown by appliance
- **Mobile Responsive**: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices

## How to Use

1. Select your system voltage (12V, 24V, or 48V)
2. Add appliances from the dropdown menu
3. Adjust quantity and hours per day for each appliance
4. Click "Calculate My Solar System" to see recommendations

## System Assumptions

- **Peak Sun Hours**: 5 hours (Nigerian average)
- **Battery Depth of Discharge**: 80%
- **System Losses**: 20%
- **Oversizing Factor**: ×1.2 (toggleable, applies to battery, inverter, and panels)
